DIY Plant Irrigation System

This is my favorite personal project—a smart irrigation system I built to take care of my plants when I’m busy with studies or sports.


How it works: Using an Arduino board, soil moisture sensors, and a small water pump, the system automatically waters plants when the soil is dry. It also sends data to my phone via Bluetooth, so I can check the status remotely.


Building process: From sketching the circuit diagram to coding the logic, it took 3 weekends. The biggest challenge was calibrating the sensor sensitivity—too sensitive and it waters too often, too low and the plants dry out!
